Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship pipes internationally via DHL. However, due to customs regulations, we cannot ship tobacco products outside of the continental United States.

How long does a bespoke commission take?

Our current waitlist is approximately 12 to 16 weeks from the time the deposit is paid and the block is selected. Quality cannot be rushed.

What is your return policy on estate pipes?

All estate pipes have a 7-day inspection period. If you are not satisfied, return it unsmoked for a full refund minus shipping costs.
