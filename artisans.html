The Hands Behind The Loom

Meet Our Master Artisans

Every WeaveNest rug is a labor of love, requiring hundreds of hours of precision, skill, and dedication. Get to know the craftspeople who turn raw fibers into heirloom masterpieces.

Amir Hassan

Master Weaver

With over 40 years at the loom, Amir specializes in the complex Persian knot. His intricate detailing ensures that every geometric and floral pattern is perfectly rendered with mathematical precision.

Leyla Tariq

Head Dye Master

Leyla is a visionary in botanical and vegetable dyes. She sources indigo, madder root, and pomegranate husks to create the rich, fade-resistant color palettes that define our heritage collections.

David Mercer

Fiber & Sourcing Specialist

David travels globally to ethically source the finest raw materials. From pure New Zealand wool to authentic mulberry silk, he ensures the foundational elements of our rugs are unparalleled in quality.

Elena Rodriguez

Creative Director

Elena translates contemporary interior design trends into actionable weaving drafts (talim). She acts as the bridge between the client's vision and the artisan's traditional technique.

Nadia Rostami

Finishing & Washing Expert

The finishing touch is crucial. Nadia oversees the antique washing and shearing processes, ensuring the pile height is perfect and the rug achieves its luxurious, soft luster before it reaches your home.

The Wider Guild

WeaveNest supports a guild of over 50 independent weavers, spinners, and dyers. We are committed to fair trade, ethical wages, and preserving this ancient art form for future generations.

The Process

How It's Made

01

Sourcing

Hand-selecting premium, long-staple wool and pure silk for maximum durability and softness.

02

Dyeing

Using botanical extracts and traditional vat methods to achieve rich, vibrant, and natural hues.

03

Knotting

Months of meticulous hand-tying on the loom, with up to 400 knots per square inch for complex motifs.

04

Finishing

Washing, stretching, and shearing the pile to bring out the rug's ultimate luster and precise texture.
