Material Selection

Explore Our Exotic Woods

The soul of a pipe lies in its material. Discover the unique smoking characteristics and visual appeal of our finest carving blocks.

Corsican Briar

Origin: France The Gold Standard

Extremely heat-resistant and highly porous. Corsican briar absorbs moisture for a dry smoke and develops a beautiful, unique patina over decades of use.

  • Durability 10/10
  • Weight Light to Medium
Shop Briar Pipes →

Olive Wood

Origin: Italy Sweet & Striking

Known for its spectacular, contrasting grain patterns. Olive wood imparts a subtle, natural sweetness to Virginia blends during the first dozen smokes.

  • Durability 8/10
  • Weight Medium
Shop Olive Pipes →

Block Meerschaum

Origin: Turkey Pure & Neutral

Not a wood, but a mineral. Meerschaum offers the truest taste of your tobacco, ghost-free, and slowly colors from stark white to a rich, warm amber.

  • Durability 6/10 (Fragile)
  • Weight Very Light
